# Phase 2.5 - Enterprise Auth and Compliance

## Identity federation

- SAML-based SSO with just-in-time or SCIM provisioned users.
- Enforce domain restrictions for tenant signup.

## Authorization extensions

- Custom role templates per workspace.
- Permission bundles by function and environment.

## Compliance controls

- Signed audit exports.
- Immutable policy history.
- Optional customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK).
